In "Invited talk" session at the 29th Meeting on Image Recognition and Understanding (MIRU2026), we will invite authors whose papers have been accepted by top conferences and top journals to share their experiences in research and paper writings that have withstood rigorous competition and peer review. We are seeking nominations for the invited talks.  
Please note that if we receive a large number of nominations, selections will be made considering the balance of fields, among other factors. The selected authors are invited to the following presentations:

- Give a 5-minute oral presentation on their experiences in research and paper writings that have withstood rigorous competition and peer review, during the invited talk session held in the mornings from August 3 to August 6, 2026. (Please note that travel and accommodation expenses will not be covered. However, if your only presentation at MIRU2026 is this invited talk, you will be exempt from the registration fee.)
- Present a poster at one of the interactive sessions held in the afternoon of the same day as your oral presentation.

\* Please note that the presentation time may be adjusted depending on the number of invited talks.

Nominations will be conducted via CMT. Please log in using the button below, and nominate the paper. Please refer to [this page](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/19_IN5E8DKTbKk_MgT4oE7dgUkC8Shaxq/edit) for instructions on how to use the system.
